About

Welcome to LSD Graphic Design — where creativity, precision, and bold ideas collide. I'm Logan Dillon, the designer behind it all. Based in Ohio, I help businesses and personal brands around the world bring their ideas to life through unforgettable logos and visuals. Whether it's an edgy startup, a legal firm, or a luxury brand, I design with clarity and confidence.

I specialize in logos, brand identity systems, and social media design. I don't use templates — everything I create is fully custom and crafted to match the client's exact vision. From minimal to bold, from modern to classic — I've done it all.
