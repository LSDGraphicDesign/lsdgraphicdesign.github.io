About

Welcome to LSD Graphic Design — where luxury aesthetics, precision, and creative vision merge. Every project I take on is a chance to tell a visual story — one that’s clean, creative, and crafted with purpose. Whether I’m building an identity for a startup or elevating an established brand, I aim to deliver sleek, professional results every time. I'm Logan Dillon, the founder and designer.
