About

Welcome to LSD Graphic Design — where luxury aesthetics, precision, and creative vision merge. I'm Logan Dillon, the founder and designer. From bold branding to refined logos, I create designs that leave lasting impressions. Based in Ohio and working globally, I specialize in crafting visual identities that feel premium, timeless, and unique.

With every project, I prioritize two key things: a fast, reliable turnaround and crystal-clear communication. My clients consistently praise how easy I am to work with — whether it’s finalizing concepts quickly, responding promptly to messages, or making revisions the same day. I make the design process simple, smooth, and collaborative.

Whether you’re a startup building from scratch or an established business ready to evolve, I’ll help you create visuals that hit hard and stay sharp. I bring your ideas to life — clean, custom, and always on point.
