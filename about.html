About

Hey, I’m Logan — the designer behind LSD Graphic Design. This isn’t just a side hustle or a hobby. This is passion-meets-professionalism, built on fast turnarounds, clean visuals, and real communication.

I started LSD Graphic Design to give brands the kind of logos and visual identity they deserve — creative, memorable, and built to scale. I’ve worked with small business owners, social media creators, and entrepreneurs who want more than a cookie-cutter logo.

My style leans modern, minimal, and bold. But it’s not about me — it’s about making your brand feel like you. If you want something that reflects your vision, works across platforms, and actually feels custom — you’re in the right place.

I respond fast, revise fast, and design even faster. If you’ve got an idea — I can bring it to life.
